# Introduction
My artwork is protected by copyright, but that doesn't mean you can't use it in any way.
Here you can learn all the conditions of sharing and using my art.

This license applies to my drawings.

# Conditions
## Allowed
- **Publicly share it with proper attribution**: Include my name, name of the artwork and optionally the source
- **Private sharing**
- **Inspiring from it**
- **Using it as reference** in your art

## Not allowed
- **Commercial use**: Selling, using it in paid content
- **Using it to train AI**
- **Pretending to be the author**
- **Removing or changing my signature**

## Permission
You can use my art under the conditions above without asking,
but I highly appreciate if you let me know! 

# What's attribution?
Attribution basically means specifying the original author of the work.
For example:
```
<image here>
"Fight the Void" - Wolfyxon
```
